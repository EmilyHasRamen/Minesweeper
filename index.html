Minesweeper

Let's play a game of Minesweeper!

Click your left mouse button to uncover a square. Click your right mouse button to flag a square.


GOAL: Safely reveal squares without hitting a red mine. If you reveal a red mine, you lose the game.


HINTS: When light gray squares are revealed, digits indicate how many adjacent squares contain mines. For example, a square anywhere in the center touches 8 squares along its sides and at its corners. If the number “3” is revealed, 3 mines are touching that square, along its side(s) or corner(s).


HOW TO WIN: Reveal all safe squares and avoid all mines to win.
